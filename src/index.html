Tools and Materials

Chisel

The chisel is a tool used for carving and shaping materials such as wood, stone, or metal. It comes in various shapes and sizes.

Hammer

Hammers are used to drive chisels and other tools. Sculptors often use wooden or metal hammers depending on the material being worked on.

Clay

Clay is a common sculpting material due to its flexibility and ease of shaping. It can be fired in a kiln to harden.
